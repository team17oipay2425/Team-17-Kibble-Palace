About Our Team

Meet the student designers and developers behind the Kibble Palace Footfall Tracking Project.

We are Team 17: Fan Xinyu, Tay Rui Qi, Chew Liang Zhi, Elroy Lee, Ng Quan Han, and Goh Jia Rui Gary. As a group of student designers and developers, we are focused on creating meaningful, low-cost solutions for real-world problems.

Our Journey

Our diverse backgrounds in engineering, design, and technology allowed us to approach this challenge from multiple perspectives. Throughout this project, we've learned the importance of balancing technological innovation with heritage preservation.

Our goal was to ensure our solution would enhance rather than compromise the historical significance of the magnificent Kibble Palace, creating a system that is both effective and respectful.

Team 17 at the Kibble Palace

Our Mission

To deliver an accurate, user-centred footfall tracking system that respects the heritage and design of the Kibble Palace while providing valuable insights for facility management.

Our Approach

User-Centred Design

We placed the needs of Glasgow City Council and Kibble Palace visitors at the heart of our process through interviews and direct feedback.

Heritage-First Technology

Our primary rule was that technology must serve and protect the heritage of the site. This led to our non-invasive, discreet solution.

Iterative Prototyping

We believe in building, testing, and learning. Our physical and digital prototypes were crucial for refining our system's accuracy and usability.

Collaborative Spirit

Our diverse skills in engineering and design allowed us to tackle challenges from every angle, leading to a more robust final product.
